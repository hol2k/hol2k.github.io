Tinker's Satchel (Risk of Rain 2)
Tinker's Satchel is a mod (third-party modification) for the video game Risk of Rain 2. It adds significant amounts of original content to various areas of the game, including over 40 new items (base game has 186) with custom models. This section of the gallery features said models.
