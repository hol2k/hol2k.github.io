Gallery Controls

Each gallery tile displays a description of a category of images. Click anywhere on a tile that has a full-page preview toggle button to open the image gallery for that category.

Within an image gallery, use the snapping horizontal scrollbar at the bottom to switch images. Click anywhere else to close the gallery (indicated by ).

Game Development
Tinker's Satchel (Risk of Rain 2)
Tinker's Satchel is a mod (third-party modification) for the video game Risk of Rain 2. It adds significant amounts of original content to various areas of the game, including over 40 new items (base game has 186) with custom models. This section of the gallery features a selection of said models.
